BABARA-BARRY

Home / Furniture / Chanintr x Designers

Design makes us tick — it’s why we get up in the morning and do what we do. The greatest gift of design is the friendships that form through connections made with the designers and their creations. Chanintr and Co is now building upon those friendships to collaborate with trusted designers and turn their ideas into products.

FRIENDSHIPS FORMED THROUGH DESIGN

As a long-time partner and friend of CHANINTR, California designer Barbara Barry was our first call. The first capsule collection of Barbara's furniture pieces is made up of 14 bestselling designs that are now environmentally crafted with certified OEKO-TEX® fabrics and updated finishes along with two new bamboo silk and wool rugs in a selection of Barbara Barry colors.

More of our friends and designers will be introduced at Chanintr and Co in the very near future.
